How to get Student developer pack

  1. Open the Git Hub Education website using any browser or click hereto go to the website.
  2. then click on signin and login with your git hub account.
  3. After that click on student developer pack.
  4. click on Sign up for Student Developer pack.
  5. Now select your college email id and college name and click on Continue.
  6. Allow your location to move on to next page.
  7. After that upload your college ID card photo
    • Incase if you didn't have your ID card upload you college allotment order.
  8. Then click on continue .Now your student developer pack reqest is approved sucessfully. Your will get the benifits within 12 days.
  9. Ater receiving the benifits you will see this type of interface as shown below. Now you can use your student developer pack.
